Description:

PCS swimming is the swimming team for PCS. The PCS Swimming team is where people from PCS go to swim, on a team. If someone from PCS swims, they probably do so for the PCS swim team. If they swim for some other team, the are dirty traitors of the PCS swim team.

Goal:

At the PCS swim team we aim to have people from PCS swim in a pool. Hopefully, the people Swimming will swim quickly. Eventually the PCS swim team members will swim in the same pool as people from swim teams of other schools. When this happens, we hope to swim faster than them. However, fun is the number one goal, or something cheesy like that.

Meetings:

The PCS swim team meets every thursday after school at the pool that we don't have. If you cant find the pool, that is on you. We also meet occasionally on other dates which are either set or not set; these are required meetings. Failure to show up for one practice will result in expulsion from the PCS swim team.
